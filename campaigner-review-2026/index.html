🔑 Key Features That Define Campaigner in 2026

⚡

Advanced Automation Workflows

Campaigner's visual workflow builder lets you create multi-step sequences triggered by opens, clicks, purchases, sign-ups, page visits, or custom events. Branch workflows based on conditions, set time delays, apply tags, move subscribers between lists, and trigger SMS alongside emails.

Enterprise-grade automation at mid-market pricing — rivals tools costing 2-3x more.

🎯

Deep Segmentation

Go beyond basic demographics. Segment by engagement history, behavioral data, geographic location, custom fields, and tags using nested AND/OR logic. Target subscribers who opened your last 3 emails AND purchased in the last 30 days AND are located in California.

Precision targeting that enterprise marketers demand.

📱

Email + SMS Marketing

SMS included in Essential ($179/month) and Advanced ($649/month) plans with 1,000 SMS sends/month on Essential. Send standalone SMS or include SMS steps within automation workflows. Supports personalization, segmentation, geolocation targeting, recurring campaigns. Free phone number included.

Note: SMS only available for US/Canadian numbers as of early 2026.

🖼️

900+ Template Library

Access 900+ responsive email templates organized by industry, occasion, and style. Drag-and-drop editor handles layout, images, buttons, and text. Full code editor available for HTML-savvy users. Dynamic content blocks automatically swap content based on subscriber attributes.

One of the largest template libraries in the industry.

📊

A/B & Multivariate Testing

Standard A/B testing on subject lines, sender names, content, and send times — plus multivariate testing allowing simultaneous testing of multiple variables. This enterprise feature is rarely available at this price point.

Systematically optimize every campaign element.

🛒

Ecommerce Integration

Native integrations with Shopify and Magento sync product catalogs, customer data, and purchase history. Trigger automation based on buying behavior — abandoned cart emails, product recommendations, win-back campaigns.

Ecommerce-specific workflows out of the box.

✅ Pros & ❌ Cons: The Honest Assessment

✅ What Campaigner Does Well

  • Automation depth at mid-market pricing — workflow builder rivals tools costing 2-3x more
  • Segmentation precision — nested AND/OR logic with behavioral, geographic, and engagement data
  • Email + SMS in one platform — eliminates need for separate SMS tool
  • Multivariate testing — rare at this price point
  • 97% deliverability rate — automatic pre-send email checking prevents delivery problems
  • Flexible contact model — some plans price by monthly volume rather than contact count
  • 900+ templates — one of the largest libraries available

❌ Where Campaigner Falls Short

  • Steep learning curve — not a "send first email in 15 minutes" tool
  • Design editor quirks — users report spacing changes, font reassignment, broken links, occasional loss of work
  • No mobile app — all campaign management on desktop only (as of early 2026)
  • Customer support gaps — slow response times, difficulty reaching knowledgeable agents
  • Limited native integrations — beyond Shopify/Magento, most require API or Zapier
  • No free version — free trial requires credit card upfront
  • SMS limited to US/Canada — not available internationally

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campaigner good for small businesses?
Campaigner is best for mid-sized businesses (2,000+ subscribers) that need advanced automation and segmentation. For very small businesses, simpler tools like Mailchimp or MailerLite may be more cost-effective and easier to use.
Does Campaigner have a free plan?
No, Campaigner does not offer a free plan. A 30-day free trial is available, but requires a credit card. Paid plans start at $59/month.
Can I use Campaigner for SMS marketing?
Yes, SMS marketing is included in the Essential ($179/month) and Advanced ($649/month) plans. It supports segmentation, automation, and personalization, but is currently limited to US and Canadian numbers.
How does Campaigner compare to Mailchimp?
Campaigner offers more advanced automation and segmentation than Mailchimp, making it better for growing businesses. Mailchimp is easier to start with and has a free plan, but its automation capabilities are more limited.
