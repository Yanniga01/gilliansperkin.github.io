<!-- Hidden Costs -->
<h2 id="hidden-costs">⚠️ Hidden Costs & Extra Charges You Need to Know</h2>
<p>The $29/month sticker price is just the beginning. Here are the real costs that most review sites don't tell you about:</p>

<h3>1. Add‑Ons Can Increase Your Monthly Bill</h3>
<p>Jobber offers several add‑ons that are not included in the base plan price. These include:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Receptionist</strong> – AI‑powered lead capture 24/7</li>
    <li><strong>Sales Pipeline</strong> – Manage deals and track opportunities</li>
    <li><strong>Campaigns</strong> – Email and SMS marketing campaigns</li>
    <li><strong>Reviews</strong> – Automate review requests and manage reputation</li>
    <li><strong>Referrals</strong> – Track and manage referral programs</li>
    <li><strong>Marketing Suite</strong> – Bundles Campaigns, Reviews, Referrals, plus Job Showcase, Social Posting, and Marketing Plan</li>
</ul>
<p>These add‑ons activate immediately and are prorated based on your billing cycle. They are also non‑refundable.</p>

<h3>2. Payment Processing Fees</h3>
<p>Jobber Payments is included, but it's not free. Credit card transactions incur a fee (typically around 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction), and rates vary by payment method. For a business processing $10,000/month in card payments, that's an extra ~$320/month.</p>

<h3>3. Instant Payouts Cost Extra</h3>
<p>If you want instant access to your funds after a job, Jobber charges an additional <strong>1% fee</strong> on the payout amount. This can add up if you rely on fast cash flow.</p>

<h3>4. Sales Tax</h3>
<p>Depending on your billing address, sales tax may be added to your subscription total. This varies by state/province and is not reflected in the advertised prices.</p>

<h3>5. Non‑Refundable Charges & Automatic Renewal</h3>
<p>Jobber charges are non‑refundable, and subscriptions automatically renew unless you cancel before the end of the current period. If you forget to cancel, you'll be billed for another term.</p>

<h3>True Cost Example: 5‑Person Team</h3>
<div style="background: #f8fafc; padding: 1.5rem; border-radius: 12px; margin: 1rem 0;">

    <ul>
        <li>Connect plan (billed annually): $89/month</li>
        <li>5 users included – no extra user fees</li>
        <li>Payment processing (2.9% + $0.30 on $8,000/month): ~$262/month</li>

        <li><strong>Total monthly cost (without add‑ons):</strong> ~$351/month</li>
        <li><strong>With Marketing Suite:</strong> ~$400–450/month</li>
    </ul>
    <p><strong>Annual cost:</strong> ~$4,200+ – significantly more than the $29 headline.</p>
</div>
