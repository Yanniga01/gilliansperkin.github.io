<!-- What Is PDFfiller? -->
<section id="what-is-pdffiller" class="content-section">
  <h2>📄 What Is PDFfiller?</h2>
  <p><strong>PDFfiller is a browser‑based PDF editor and document management platform</strong> that allows you to edit PDFs, create fillable forms, collect e‑signatures, send contracts online, manage cloud documents, and automate paperwork workflows.</p>
  <p>Unlike traditional desktop software, PDFfiller works entirely online. You can use it on Windows, Mac, Android, iPhone, and tablets without installing heavy software.</p>
  <p>For many small businesses, freelancers, agencies, and remote teams, it replaces older workflows involving printing documents, scanning papers, emailing attachments repeatedly, and manually signing forms.</p>
  <p>For a deeper look at pricing and ROI, see our <a href="/pdffiller-pricing-2026/">PDFfiller Pricing 2026 guide</a>.</p>
</section>

<section id="before-you-start" class="content-section">
  <h2>⚡ Before You Start: What You Actually Need</h2>
  <p>You only need a browser, an internet connection, and a PDFfiller account. Google Chrome usually performs best, though Safari and Edge also work well.</p>
  <p><strong>Important trial note:</strong> PDFfiller offers a free trial with access to premium tools, but it requires a payment card upfront and the subscription auto‑renews after the trial ends. A lot of users forget this part – which is one reason many people later search for <a href="/pdffiller-alternatives/">PDFfiller alternatives</a>. If you’re testing the platform, <strong>set a reminder before the billing date</strong>.</p>
  <p>If you’re still deciding whether PDFfiller is right for your business, read our <a href="/pdffiller-small-business-review/">in‑depth analysis of PDFfiller for small businesses</a> first.</p>
</section>

<!-- Step 1: Upload Your First Document -->
<section id="step1" class="content-section">
  <h2>1. 📂 Upload Your First Document</h2>
  <p>After logging in, you’ll land on the <strong>My Docs</strong> dashboard – your digital filing cabinet. Here, uploaded documents, templates, signed files, forms, and shared documents are stored.</p>
  <p>You have three main ways to add files:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Upload From Your Device:</strong> Click the green “Add Document” button. PDFfiller supports PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, JPG, PNG. Non‑PDF files automatically convert into editable PDFs during upload.</li>
    <li><strong>Import From Cloud Storage:</strong> Connect Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, or Box directly. This removes the annoying download → edit → re‑upload cycle.</li>
    <li><strong>Use the Built‑In Form Library:</strong> PDFfiller includes millions of tax forms, HR documents, real estate contracts, legal agreements, and healthcare paperwork. Search before creating from scratch – the form may already exist.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<!-- Step 2: Edit Your PDF -->
<section id="step2" class="content-section">
  <h2>2. ✏️ Edit Your PDF</h2>
  <p>Once your document opens, you’ll see the editing toolbar across the top. Here are the essential tools for beginners:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Add Text:</strong> Click the Text tool, then click anywhere on the document. A text box appears where you can type, resize, change fonts, and adjust colours.</li>
    <li><strong>Edit Existing Text:</strong> Enable “Edit PDF” mode, then click existing text to modify it. This only works on editable PDFs – scanned images require OCR or overlay text.</li>
    <li><strong>Add Images and Logos:</strong> The Image tool allows you to insert signatures, logos, stamps, and branding elements – perfect for invoices and branded contracts.</li>
    <li><strong>Highlight and Annotate:</strong> Use highlighting, underlining, comment boxes, and strike‑through tools for contract reviews and collaboration.</li>
    <li><strong>Erase Content:</strong> The Erase tool removes visible text or sections cleanly – great for modifying old templates.</li>
    <li><strong>Add Headers, Footers, and Page Numbers:</strong> Automatically add page numbers, dates, and headers across all pages for professional contracts and reports.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>When finished, click the orange <strong>Done</strong> button to save changes.</p>
</section>

<!-- Step 3: Create Fillable Forms -->
<section id="step3" class="content-section">
  <h2>3. 📝 Create Fillable Forms</h2>
  <p>If you want other people to complete the document digitally, you’ll need fillable fields. Open “Add Fillable Fields” to insert text boxes, checkboxes, dropdown menus, date selectors, and signature fields. Simply drag them onto the document.</p>
  <p><strong>The Smart Shortcut: Auto Detect Fields</strong> – Instead of manually placing every field, click “Detect Fields”. The system analyzes the document layout and automatically suggests field locations. For standard forms, this saves huge amounts of time. Always review the placements manually, but it dramatically accelerates setup.</p>
</section>

<!-- Step 4: Sign Documents Digitally -->
<section id="step4" class="content-section">
  <h2>4. ✍️ Sign Documents Digitally</h2>
  <p>PDFfiller supports legally binding electronic signatures compliant with ESIGN regulations. You have three signing methods:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Draw Signature:</strong> Use your mouse, touchpad, or finger on mobile – feels most natural.</li>
    <li><strong>Type Signature:</strong> Type your name and choose a signature font – fastest for business use.</li>
    <li><strong>Upload Signature Image:</strong> Upload a clean PNG or JPG of your handwritten signature – creates the most polished appearance.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>After your first signature, PDFfiller stores it automatically for future use.</p>
</section>

<!-- Step 5: Send Documents for Signature -->
<section id="step5" class="content-section">
  <h2>5. 📨 Send Documents for Signature</h2>
  <p>This is where PDFfiller becomes genuinely powerful for business workflows. After editing, click Done → “Send to Sign”. Enter recipient emails, assign fields, and send. Recipients don’t need accounts – they open documents directly in browser, sign digitally, and submit instantly. You receive notifications automatically when completed.</p>
  <p>For contracts involving multiple people, you can assign fields to different signers, control signing order, and track completion status. This removes endless email attachment chains.</p>
  <p>For a full comparison of e‑signature options, see <a href="/adobe-acrobat-vs-pdffiller/">Adobe Acrobat vs PDFfiller</a>.</p>
</section>

<!-- Step 6: Create Templates -->
<section id="step6" class="content-section">
  <h2>6. 🔁 Create Templates</h2>
  <p>If you repeatedly send contracts, NDAs, onboarding forms, invoices, or agreements, templates become a massive productivity upgrade. To create one: open the document, click the three‑dot menu, and select “Make Template”. Now every time you use it, PDFfiller creates a fresh editable copy automatically. This alone can save hours every week for small businesses.</p>
</section>

<!-- Step 7: Organize Your Documents -->
<section id="step7" class="content-section">
  <h2>7. 📁 Organize Your Documents Properly</h2>
  <p>Most beginners ignore organization until their dashboard becomes chaotic. PDFfiller includes several helpful tools:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Folders:</strong> Create folders by client, project, year, or document type – just like a computer file system.</li>
    <li><strong>Tags:</strong> Use hashtags like #legal, #invoice, #client, #2026Q2. Later, you can instantly search by hashtag – extremely useful as your document library grows.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<!-- Step 8: AI Features (2026) -->
<section id="step8" class="content-section">
  <h2>8. 🤖 Use the AI Features (2026)</h2>
  <p>PDFfiller introduced several AI tools that significantly improve workflow speed:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>AI PDF Editor:</strong> Describe what you need (e.g., “Create a freelance service agreement with payment terms and revision policy”). The AI generates a structured document within seconds – perfect for freelancers, agencies, and startups.</li>
    <li><strong>Chat With PDF:</strong> Upload a document and ask questions like “What are the payment terms?” or “Does this contract include a non‑compete clause?” Instead of manually reading dozens of pages, the AI extracts the answer instantly.</li>
    <li><strong>AI Summaries:</strong> Long contracts and reports can be summarised automatically – extremely useful before meetings, negotiations, or client calls when you need a fast refresher.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<!-- Common Beginner Mistakes -->
<section id="mistakes" class="content-section">
  <h2>⚠️ Common Beginner Mistakes to Avoid</h2>
  <div class="pros-cons-grid">
    <div class="pros-box">
      <h3>✅ Do This Instead</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>Use fillable fields for forms – not floating text boxes</li>
        <li>Click “Done” after editing to properly finalise changes</li>
        <li>Assign signature fields before sending documents</li>
        <li>Create templates for documents you reuse weekly</li>
        <li>Set a calendar reminder for the trial end date</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="cons-box">
      <h3>❌ Avoid These Mistakes</h3>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Using text overlay instead of fillable fields</strong> – if a form already has interactive fields, type directly into them</li>
        <li><strong>Forgetting to click “Done”</strong> – autosave exists, but Done finalises the editing session properly</li>
        <li><strong>Sending documents without assigned fields</strong> – recipients may receive confusing blank documents</li>
        <li><strong>Ignoring templates</strong> – most regular users waste hours rebuilding documents manually</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
