<h3>❌ Skip Semrush if:</h3>
<ul>
  <li>You're a solo blogger with limited traffic – SE Ranking ($52/month) or Mangools ($29/month) cover your needs.</li>
  <li>Your primary need is backlink analysis – Ahrefs Lite ($129/month) is better and cheaper.</li>
  <li>You're on a tight budget and won't use more than 3 features – you're paying for breadth you don't need.</li>
  <li>You're in the first year of your site with no revenue – invest the money in content or links first, then get Semrush.</li>
