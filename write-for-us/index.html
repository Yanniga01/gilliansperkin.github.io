Guest Post Requirements

1

Original Content Only

Your submission must be original and unpublished. We check for plagiarism.

2

Depth & Helpfulness

We prefer detailed guides: typically 1,500–2,500 words, depending on the topic.

3

Practical, Real-World Advice

Use examples, step-by-step workflows, and clear recommendations for small businesses.

4

Sources When You Claim Facts

If you reference stats, benchmarks, or pricing changes, include sources we can verify.

5

Clean Formatting

Use headings, bullet points, short paragraphs, and (optional) relevant screenshots or images.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you pay for guest posts?

No. Guest posts are unpaid. The benefit is exposure, credibility, and an author bio link (subject to approval).

Can I include affiliate links?

No. We don’t accept affiliate links in guest posts.

How long does the review process take?

Pitch response is usually within 48 business hours. Draft review can take 5–7 business days.

Can I republish the article elsewhere?

No. Accepted articles are exclusive to SmartGuideHubs. You may share an excerpt with a link to the original.

What format do you accept?

Google Docs or Word. If you include images, add them as separate files or links with proper attribution.

Do you accept previously published content?

No. Only original, unpublished content is accepted.
